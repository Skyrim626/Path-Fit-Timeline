The initial encounter with our teacher, Ma'am Shantilly, occured on this day. She introduced herself as our PATH-FIT instructor for the entire second semester, and then initiated a game for us to become acquainted with each other. The game was called Wink Murder, which is also known as murder wink, killer, or killer killer, Our group consisted of four members because some of our classmates were absent that day. To play the game, our instructor distributed papers containing different roles, and we randomly selected our roles. The roles included victims, a detective, and a murderer, with the latter tasked with eliminating everyone before being caught by the detective. Initially, I was a bit confused about the game's rules, but my confusion was cleared up when on of my fellow memers winked at me to signal the start of the game. We all found the game to be a bit confusing but entertaining. In the second round, I was fortunate enough to be assigned the role of the murderer, and I won the game with little effort.
